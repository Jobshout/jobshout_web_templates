Work For us

Recruitment Solution is an ambitious, fast growing specialist in Social Work and Social Care recruitment. As such we are always looking to hire aspiring or experienced recruitment consultants to join our team. Whilst we do not specifically ask for you to have a background in recruitment we do need prospective staff members to have the following attributes –

  • Drive
  • Determination
  • An overall desire to succeed
  • Sales Acumen
  • Ability to thrive under pressure
  • Ability to work to sales targets
  • Degree Educated or proven Sales Experience

We offer a professional yet fun working atmosphere which is designed for motivated individuals to be able to grow and develop with us through a structured career path. Our purpose designed offices in central Reading will accommodate our growth over the next 3 years as Recruitment Solution becomes a leader in its field.  You could not join us at a better time. We also offer unrivalled employee benefits including -

  • Very competitive basic salary 
  • Excellent commission scheme (uncapped)
  • Annual bonus scheme against your earnings (uncapped)
  • 25 days annual leave + all bank holidays as standard which increases with tenure 
  • Extra days annual leave for your birthday
  • Private Medical Insurance (subject to length of service)
  • Contributory Pension Scheme
  • Employee Assistance Service
  • 5pm finish on a Friday
  • Dress down day every Friday
  • Quarterly events (fine dining, outdoor events / activities)
  • Regular internal training and development
  • APSCo approved external training and development

To top it off you are joining a growing business which you will be part of and grow with rapidly.

If you are looking for a recruitment job in Reading and would like to apply to work for Recruitment Solution then send your CV and a covering email to jobs@recruitmentsolution.com quoting ‘Internal Recruitment’.  We look forward to hearing from you!
