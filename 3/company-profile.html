About Recruitment Solution -The Social Work and Social Care recruitment agency

Recruitment Solution is a specialist high end social work and social care recruitment agency. Established in 2009, we pride ourselves on being an independently owned specialist who has your needs at the heart of what we do.

As specialist social work and social care recruiters we are able to offer a market leading temporary and permanent recruitment service, providing career enhancing opportunities throughout the UK.

We provide staffing solutions to all organisations within our specialist market including Local Authorities, Private & Voluntary organisations and NHS / Healthcare services.

Whether you are looking for a new job or you are looking for staff we would be delighted to hear from you and discuss how our professional recruitment services can be of assistance.

For more details and for professional recruitment advice please contact us.
