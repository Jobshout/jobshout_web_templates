Key Features

Let Jobshout build your online reputation while you concentrate on building your business.

Jobshout is a reliable, effective and fully customisable software solution which:

  • Builds a database of active and passive candidates.
  • Optimises content for natural search engine rankings - reducing your Google AdWord spend.
  • Integrates fully with Broadbean software.
  • Automatically posts your adverts to a range of social media platforms.
  • Allows candidates to apply directly to jobs using their LinkedIn profiles.
  • Provides a reliable online presence - externally hosted on Jobshout’s secure server.
  • Is quick & easy to set-up and use.

Jobshout  - Software as a Service - and a solution.

Jobshout is built around using ‘Software as a Service’ (SAAS), web-based on-demand software which means Jobshout can:

  • Set-up and maintain a branded website for your recruitment business or HR department.
  • Remove the need for you in invest in expensive servers, database licenses or proprietary technology.
  • Provide your recruitment business with a Cloud Based Computing solution, backed up by years of web development and cloud-based computing experience.
  • Give you the means to create a fully branded, professional job board within minutes

Let Jobshout transform your online presence and streamline your workflow with:

  • Search Engine Optimisation (SEO): Jobshout has a built-in SEO manager which automatically optimises job adverts for search engine ranking - reducing the need for additional job board spend. 
  • Data Organisation: Jobshout Organises job adverts by market sectors, locations and skills. And CVs by candidate experience and interest profiles.
  • Remote Access - Jobshout’s Secure online access allows recruitment professionals to manage their workflow, appointments and candidates when away from the office.
  • Streamlined online publishing: Jobshout automatically pushes job adverts to 3rd party job boards including monster.com, Reed.co.uk and Jobserve.com via BroadBean AdCourier technology.
  • Automated processes: Customisable automated processes for both users and administrators. Including instant pushing of job adverts to social platforms, remote database servers & other external systems.
  • CV handler & parser: Jobshout’s automated indexing process makes searching CV’s simple and swift in any format - Word, RTF or PDF.
  • Email Queue: Jobshout manages email job alerts, auto task responses, invitations and running campaigns. 
  • Site traffic analyser: In-built real time site performance analytics.
  • Job brief submission system: Enables clients and potential clients to upload vacancy details directly into Jobshout.
  • Dynamic RSS feed generator - Matched to job seeker’s specific search terms.
  • Content repository: Enables management of multiple domain micro-sites.
  • Content Management System (CMS) - Jobshout’s in-built CMS ensures all content displayed online is professionally branded and formatted.
  • Jobshout Dashboard - Jobshot’s ‘back-end’ administration sites are fully integrated with front-end website ensuring easy maintenance of all aspects of a recruiter’s online presence.
