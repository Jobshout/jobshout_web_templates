We are committed to provide world class Technical Support.

Jobshout provides top quality technical support for all of our technological products and software solutions, catering to industries such as Publishing, Recruitment, HR, Telecom, Banking, Financial Services and several other sectors. We claim with ample justification that our technical support team can certainly resolve any issue in a matter of couple hours.

For Mobile Apps ( including iPhone and iPad ) Technical Support please contact us by filling the form at Contact Us

For CatBase Technical Support please visit http://www.catbase.com/catbase-technical-support-and-training.html

For Jobshout Technical Support please visit http://www.jobshout.com/support.html

We can also arrange for tailer made SLA contracts for clients.

We also offer 24/7 support on all 365 days to make sure that all our clients are fully satisfied. We offer 4 types or levels of support namely Level 1, Level 2, Level 3, Level 4 and Level 5. The services will vary based on the charge and the package the client selects. Time and material, retainer engagement, block hours and managed services are some of types of services we currently offer to our clients, with respect to engaging our employees for the technical support contract.

Jobshout also provides Remote level assistance to clients upon request. We have many repeat clients and this is ample evidence to show our trustworthiness and reliability.

We are reliable, honest, transparent and very outspoken and it is precisely these qualities that draw our customers to us repeatedly. Call us now to know how we may be able to help your company for wide-ranging technical support services!
